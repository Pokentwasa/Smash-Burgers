THE PHILOSOPHY

Every patty is hand-smashed on a scorching flat-top at 200°C, pressed thin until the edges go crispy and the cheese melts into the crust.

No gimmicks. No shortcuts. Just heat, pressure, and obsession. We believe a burger should be simple, executed perfectly, and served fast. That's it. That's the whole philosophy.

HOW WE SMASH

01

SOURCE

100% grass-fed beef from local farms. Ground fresh daily, never frozen, seasoned with sea salt only.

02

BALL

Hand-portioned into tight balls. Same weight every time. Consistency is everything.

03

SMASH

Placed on a 200°C flat-top and pressed flat with a heavy press. 45 seconds. The edges go crispy. The Maillard reaction does the rest.

04

STACK

Cheese melted on the patty, never cold. Built bottom-up: sauce, pickles, patties, onion, bun. Every layer intentional.
